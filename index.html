üí° What is the Past Continuous?

We use the Past Continuous to talk about actions that were **in progress** (happening) in the past. Think of it as a snapshot of an action that hadn't finished yet!

1. At a Specific Time

An action happening at a *precise moment* in the past.

I was playing volleyball üèê yesterday at 6 p.m.

2. Interrupted Action

A longer action interrupted by a shorter one. We often use when.

She was listening to Adele üé∂ when I called her.

3. Two Actions Together

Two actions happening at the same time. We often use while.

I was dreaming about Italy ‚úàÔ∏è while my mom was cooking.
